The MoveWise Homeownership Blueprint

MoveWise Homeownership Preparation Program

Your personalized roadmap from preparation to keys.

Overview

Homeownership does not start when you find a house. It starts with preparation. The MoveWise Homeownership Preparation Program is designed for future homeowners who need a clear strategy, financial guidance, and accountability to become mortgage-ready.

Through personalized planning, credit readiness guidance, and a structured timeline, we help you create a realistic path toward homeownership.

Choose Your Preparation Timeline

3-Month Home Ready Plan

For buyers who are almost ready to purchase

Includes:

  • 1-on-1 Home Buyer Strategy Session
  • Credit assessment and improvement recommendations with credit team
  • Review of current financial position
  • Personalized 90-day action plan
  • Monthly progress check-ins
  • Home buying education guide
  • Lender preparation checklist
  • Buyer readiness evaluation

Goal: Improve your readiness and position yourself to begin the buying process within 3 months.

6-Month Home Ready Plan

For buyers who need more time to prepare

Includes:

  • Everything in the 3-Month Plan
  • Extended credit improvement guidance
  • Monthly accountability sessions
  • Debt reduction strategy discussion
  • Savings and budgeting plan
  • Homeownership timeline tracking
  • Neighborhood and affordability planning
  • Preparation for mortgage pre-approval

Goal: Build a stronger financial foundation and create a clear path toward purchasing a home.
