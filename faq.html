FAQ

Q: How do we pay for the products and services?

Zelle, Apple Pay, or Cash App are the only 3 preferred payment methods at this time.

Q: Who is MoveWise Agent Coaching for?

MoveWise coaching is designed for new and developing real estate agents who want structure, confidence, and consistent closings. It is for agents who are serious about building a real business.

Q: Do I already need to be licensed to join?

No. Pre-licensed individuals can join to prepare, but the coaching is most powerful for active agents ready to start generating leads and working with clients.

Q: What will I actually learn in coaching?

You will learn the practical side of real estate that most programs do not teach, including:

  • Lead generation strategies that actually work
  • How to convert conversations into clients
  • Listing and buyer consultation structure
  • Social media marketing for real estate
  • Daily systems to stay consistent and productive

Q: How is this different from real estate school or other courses?

Real estate school teaches you how to pass the exam. MoveWise coaching teaches you how to actually build a business, get clients, and close deals in the real world.

Q: How quickly can I see results?

Results depend on consistency and execution, but most agents begin seeing momentum within 30-60 days when they apply the systems consistently.

Q: What's the difference in coaching selections?

Group Coaching is for Realtor friends, family, or teams who want to do this together. One-on-One is private coaching that offers more personalized strategies and support. VIP Intensive includes private coaching, field activities, digital tools, and more.

Q: What is the homebuyer workshop?

The MoveWise Homebuyer workshop is a fully refundable guided experience designed to help buyers understand the home purchasing process from start to finish without confusion, pressure, or overwhelm. This is for home buyers who are not fully ready to buy yet due to needing credit help and financial guidance. This workshop offers a 6-month plan and a 12+ month plan.

Q: When do I get refunded for the homebuyer workshop?

After closing when you get the keys to your home, depending on your plan. If you do not close within your plan expiration date, or 30 days after, your refund will not be refunded.

Q: Can you provide advice for any of my current transactions?

We cannot advise on specific contracts or transactions for agents as this constitutes acting as a broker without authority.

Q: Is the coaching a brokerage service through Norluxe Realty?

No, this is an independent coaching service through MoveWise LLC and not a licensed real estate activity.

Q: How do I pay for the homebuying workshop?

You will pay Norluxe Realty directly through the link provided.

Q: What should I bring or prepare for the homebuying workshop?

Just bring your questions and an idea of your homebuying timeline. If you have credit or financial concerns, those are welcomed too.
